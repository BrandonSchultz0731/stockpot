How It Works

From pantry to plate
in three steps.

1

Stock your pantry

Add items manually, scan a receipt, or use the barcode scanner. StockPot tracks quantities, expiration dates, and storage locations.

2

Generate your plan

Tell StockPot your dietary needs and goals. It builds a full week of meals using ingredients you already have, minimizing waste and trips to the store.

3

Cook & track

Follow step-by-step recipes, mark meals as cooked to auto-deduct from your pantry, and let StockPot handle the shopping list for what's missing.
