Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Introduction

ChefPixel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using ChefPixel, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, first name, and optionally your last name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo URL from those services.

Dietary & Health Information: You may voluntarily provide dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free), excluded ingredients, household size, cooking skill level, and nutritional goals (daily calorie, protein, carb, and fat targets). This information is used solely to personalize your recipe and meal plan recommendations.

Pantry & Food Data: Items you add to your pantry, including ingredient names, quantities, storage locations, and expiration dates. This data is used to generate recipes and meal plans tailored to what you have on hand.

Recipes & Meal Plans: Recipes you save, meal plans you generate, and shopping lists created from those plans.

Chat Messages: Conversations with our AI kitchen assistant are stored to provide conversation history and improve the relevance of responses.

Images: Photos of grocery receipts or food items you upload for scanning. These images are processed to extract ingredient information and are not retained after processing.

Device Information: We collect device tokens for push notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) and basic session information to manage your login state.

Usage Data: We track feature usage counts (e.g., number of recipes generated, receipt scans) and token consumption for managing service limits.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Generate personalized recipes and meal plans based on your pantry, dietary preferences, and nutritional goals
  • Process receipt and barcode scans to populate your pantry
  • Power AI-assisted kitchen chat with context about your ingredients and preferences
  • Send push notifications about expiring pantry items and meal reminders (with your consent)
  • Manage your account, sessions, and subscription tier
  • Enforce usage limits and prevent abuse

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate ChefPixel:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Your pantry data, dietary preferences, and chat messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate recipes, meal plans, and chat responses. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not send your email, name, or account credentials to Anthropic.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google): Used to deliver push notifications to your device. Google receives your device token but not your personal information.
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In: If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, authentication is handled by those providers according to their respective privacy policies. We receive only your name, email, and profile information necessary to create your account.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12. Authentication tokens are cryptographically signed and refresh tokens are hashed before storage. We use HTTPS for all data transmission.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data — including your pantry items, meal plans, saved recipes, chat history, sessions, and usage records — is permanently deleted from our servers. Recipes you created that were not saved by other users are preserved in an anonymized form (with no link to your account).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View your profile, pantry, recipes, meal plans, and chat history within the app
  • Update your data: Edit your dietary profile, nutritional goals, and pantry items at any time
  • Delete your account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen in the app
  • Revoke social sign-in: When you delete your account, we revoke your Apple Sign In token. You can also manage app permissions directly through your Apple ID or Google Account settings.

Children's Privacy

ChefPixel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for expiring pantry items and meal reminders. You can manage your notification preferences within the app or disable them entirely through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the app or on our website. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
